semiya kesari

Ingredients

  • semiya
  • sugar
  • ghee
  • dry fruits
  • food colour
  • water

Instructions

  1. first take a semiya and rost it
  2. next take a water into the bowle
  3. pour the semiya into the water and cook for 10 minites
  4. next add a sugar and mix it well
  5. then add a food colour
  6. rost the dry fruits in a ghee then add a dry fruits in a semiya
  7. finally semiya kesari is ready
